OUR ALLIANCE

UCL Guild is an alliance between five entities: UCL Business Society, UCL Data Science Society, UCL Entrepreneurs Society, UCL Investment Society, UCL Women in Finance Society.
Alone, each of our societies have enjoyed great success. But together, we are capable of so much more. You are invited to join us in reaping the rewards of bigger and better opportunities and events, made possible through the close collaboration on which we are founded.
