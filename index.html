About Us

Our Alliance

UCL Guild is the largest and fastest growing society at UCL. Our aim is to give our 10,000+ members the necessary tools to kick-start their career in the best possible way. Established in 2016 and affiliated to UCL Student Union in 2017, UCL Guild is an alliance between 5 of the largest societies at UCL:
+ UCL Business Society
+ UCL Data Science Society
+ UCL Entrepreneurs
+ UCL Investment Society
+ UCL Women in Finance Society


Alone, each of our societies have enjoyed great success. But together, we form the largest society in UCL and are capable of so much more. You are invited to join us in reaping the rewards of bigger and better opportunities and events, made possible through the close collaboration on which we are founded.


" THE UCL GUILD IS DEDICATED TO THE EDUCATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF ALL ITS MEMBERS,
FOCUSING ON ALL OF THE MAJOR CAREERS "

Graduate Jobs & Recruitment Experts

Give A Grad A Go is the UK’s leading graduate recruitment firm, pairing graduates with a graduate job they’ll love, in a company that’s going places. Our core aim is to support graduates as they enter the world of work and to help them secure that graduate job they have worked so hard for throughout their studies.

We’ve been hard at work for the last 7 years, getting to know some of the most exciting, niche and innovative companies all over London and the UK. Since 2009, we have launched over 2000 graduate careers, placing our candidates into businesses ranging from London’s hottest start-ups to globally renowned brands, across a wide range of sectors.

So whether you are looking for your dream job or just general careers advice - check out our Graduate Jobs or feel free to contact the Give A Grad A Go team of graduate recruitment experts at uclguildcareers@giveagradago.com.
